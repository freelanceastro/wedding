About Us

Adam Becker

is a freelance astrophysicist, a gentleman scholar, and a lover of small mammals and bicycles. He is writing a book about the sordid untold history of quantum physics, and specializes in the universe and open science.

Elisabeth Seng

is a writer & culture maven at a boutique ad agency. She loves great books, cute fuzzy creatures, biking, and hiking.

Our Big Adventure

Adam and Elisabeth met in February 2009 in San Francisco. He was visiting California over spring break from his physics PhD program in Ann Arbor, Michigan. She had just moved to Oakland six months earlier for her first job in advertising. They met through a mutual friend, their eyes met and something happened - they couldn’t stop talking. They stayed up talking until 3am.

Adam flew back to Michigan the next day, and the romance began over instant messenger soon after. By May 2009, they were officially an item. The next three years, they dated long-distance across three time zones, logging thousands of cross-country airline miles and phone minutes. They felt that were standing on the edge of something really big— and although the distance was difficult some days, they also couldn’t walk away from a great thing. So when Adam finished his PhD in 2012, he packed all of his worldy possessions into a Toyota and drove across the country to San Francisco.

Now that they’ve been living in the same time zone for three years, they know this is a sure thing. Adam and Elisabeth are ready to tie the knot and become a family—and they’re excited for you to celebrate with them.
